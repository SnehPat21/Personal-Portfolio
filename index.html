About Me

I am currently a junior from Cedar Falls, IA at the University of Iowa majoring in Finance and Business Analytics in the Tippie College of Business. Currently, I serve as the president for the Tippie Technology and Innovation Association. Additionally, I am on the executive board for the South Asian Student Alliance and have previously served on the board for the Tippie Real Estate Club. Through my previous experiences, I have been able to develop skills such as leadership, working effectively in a team, time management, and professional presentations.
