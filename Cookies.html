What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to remember you and your preferences, either for a single visit (through a 'session cookie') or for multiple repeated visits (using a 'persistent cookie').

Disabling Cookies

You can usually prevent your browser from accepting cookies or delete cookies that have already been placed. The methods for doing this vary from browser to browser, and from version to version. You can find out more about cookie management at https://www.aboutcookies.org/.
